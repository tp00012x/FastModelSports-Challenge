Instructions

    1. Please open Console
    2. To run the App click on the green button bellow
    3. Type the followings commands to navigate through the cash register app.
      • "Show" - Show how much money you have in the cash register. By default you have 5 bills of $20, $10, $5, $2 and $1, totalling $190
      • "Take" - Take money out from the register.
      • "Change" - Take a specific change from the register
      • "Exit" - Quit Cash Register Application
